Game Description

Candy Crush Saga is a free-to-play mobile match-3 puzzle video game released by King. The goal is to swap and match colorful candies to progress through various levels and challenge friends to beat your score.

In this game, you'll crush candies, solve puzzles, and collect rewards to advance through over 1000 levels of fun!
